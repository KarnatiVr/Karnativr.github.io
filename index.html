Audisankara College of Engineering and Technology (ASCET) is a prominent engineering institution located in Gudur, Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 2001, the college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTUA), and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). ASCET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, as well as management studies. The college is known for its well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on research and innovation. It also provides numerous extracurricular activities and professional development opportunities to enhance the overall educational experience of its students
